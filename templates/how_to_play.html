How to Play

Aim

The aim of the game is to make it through the maze of tunnels before the timer runs out, or you are hit by one of the enemies. BEWARE however, that some of the tunnels are collapsed and you will not be able to get through them. You will need to navigate through the correct path to make it out safely. You will receive a score once you complete the maze. Check your score on the leaderboard here.

Controls

  • Up: Up Arrow Key
  • Down: Down Arrow Key
  • Left: Left Arrow Key
  • Right: Right Arrow Key
  • Shield (One time use): Space Bar
  • Cheat: F Key
